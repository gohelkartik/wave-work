Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a fee to join?

Signing up and posting a project is completely free. We only charge a small service fee when a payment is processed.

How does WorkWave Shield work?

We hold the funds for a project in escrow. This ensures the freelancer is motivated and the buyer is protected.

How do I become a Pro Seller?

Maintain high ratings, complete projects on time, and pass our identity verification process to earn the Pro badge.

What if I'm not satisfied?

Our dispute resolution team is available to mediate any issues and ensure a fair outcome for both parties.
